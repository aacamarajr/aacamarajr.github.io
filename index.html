Antonio CÂMARA

Postdoctoral Researcher

UNICAMP, FEA ,LMQA, Brazil

PhD Food Biotechnology

UBFC/AgroSup Dijon, France

Has large experience in food microbiology, fermentation, biotechnology and bioprocess with emphasis in funcional preservation of yeasts, biochemical/biophysical stress responses and micro-encapsulation process of bioactive compounds into microbial cells.

Currently as visiting Postdoctoral Researcher at Laboratoire Écologie Systématique Évolution, ESE, site Institut Diversité Ecologie et Evolution du Vivant, IDEEV.

Research interests

  • Preservation of microorganisms
  • Biochemical and biophysical responses to environmental stresses
  • Bioprocesses using yeast as models
  • Microencapsulation of bioactive substances in cells
  • Predictive microbiology
  • Comparative genomics
